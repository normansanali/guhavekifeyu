Did Gary Russell Jr Brother Pass Away?

Gary Russell Jr lost his brother Gary Boosa Russell on December 13, 2020, to a sudden heart attack. That very night he posted a video on Instagram showing and thanking the people who came to support him and his family during a difficult time.

The cardiac arrest came weeks before Christmas.

Another one of his brothers died way before his time should have been up. His older brother Devaun Drayton was murdered in Washington, D.C., in 2004, and it took until 2017 for an arrest to be made.

Though the deaths of his brothers rocked him to his very core, he has stayed vital for his family and has grown as a man.

Family Of Gary Russell Jr – Who Are His Brothers And Parents?

Gary Russell Jr was born into a large family with five brothers to keep him company. After their father Gary Russell, Sr., trained four of them to compete in boxing, they are all named Gary.

Gary Antuanne Russell,  Gary Allen Russell III, Gary Boosa Russell, and Gary Antonio Russell are his four brothers. Besides Gary Boosa Russell, all his brothers are Golden Gloves National Champions.

Gary Russell Sr. was their father and the longtime head trainer of him and his brothers. His father had to have one of his feet amputated last month owing to complications from type 2 diabetes.

Although the 62-year-old trainer trained Russell Jr. and the rest of his kids throughout their amateur and professional careers, he has been unable to attend most of Russell Jr.’s training sessions for the Magsayo match in suburban Washington, D.C.

Gary Russell Jr is on Instagram under the username @mrgaryrusselljr with over 67K followers.

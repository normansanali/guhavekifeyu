A Look At 'Bachelorette' Star Hannah Brown's Relationship With Her Dad

September 2024 · 4 minute read

When it comes to finding the love of her life, Hannah Brown has tried everything. She appeared on The Bachelorettes season 15 and even attempted to join a fancy dating app.

Aside from the fact she's on the Bachelor spin-off, there's something so relatable about Brown, whether she's moving into a new apartment in L.A. or talking about the relationship that she has with her parents. Let's take a look at how close Hannah is with her dad.

Support System

Before starring on the reality show that made her super famous, Hannah Brown was in the beauty pageant world. And no matter what she has done, she has always had her parents' love.

Hannah Brown's mom and dad have been her support system throughout her time in the Bachelor universe, and they have been cool with her going on reality TV to try to find love. That is awesome to hear, as it's easy to see that not every parent would be comfortable with that type of situation.

Brown's dad, Robert, said of his daughter, "She’s just so comfortable in her own skin. We’re just so proud of her," according to Heavy.com. Her mom, Susanne, posted a video on Instagram that proved how much he cares. With a scene from Hannah's time on The Bachelorette on the TV screen behind him, he said, “My daughter, Hannah Brown, the Bachelorette."

According to Heavy.com, Brown shared a sweet family photo on her Instagram and talked about how much she loved her parents in the caption.

She wrote, “Family is everything. I’ve been home for a bit recently and it feels really good to be with my people who have been with me through. it. all. Our families didn’t sign up for this. We are the ones who said yes to the vulnerable opportunity to find love this way. But thank you to the people who loved us first, and continue to support us on this journey of love, heartbreak, and life lessons!”

A Huge Inspiration

Hannah Brown said her parents' marriage is an inspiration to her. According to Heavy.com, she shared that she would love to have the same kind of relationship that they do.

According to Distractify.com, Brown shared more about the kind of partner she wants. She said, "Ultimately I want at the end of this to know that they know me. And know every part of me. And not just some version of myself that I, like, package together and am like, 'Do you like this?' And I think I've done that in the past. But the good and the bad and I want that in return."

Brown's Interviews

Hannah's dad is no stranger to the media, and he has given some interviews where he talks about his daughter's love life and time on reality TV.

Robert Brown has even weighed in on the relationship between Hannah Brown and Tyler Cameron. Robert said that they haven't rekindled their flame and Brown and Cameron are "friends."

According to Life & Style Mag, Brown said, “I don’t know if I’d officially say they were back together… They’re just like two kids.” He continued, "That’s all it is to it. Nothing more, nothing less. People want to read into whatever they want to read into, and we can’t stop that. But the reality is they’re friends.”

Brown also told Life & Style that he doesn't think that his daughter would be interested in being part of Bachelor in Paradise. He explained that the casual dating behavior on the show, along with all the alcohol, just isn't "her style, her ambition."

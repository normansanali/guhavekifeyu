Shakti Mohan Personal Information

Shakti Mohan was born on 12th October 1985 to a middle-class Hindu family in Delhi. Her father Brijmohan Sharma is a government employee and her mother Kusum Mohan is a housewife. She got her early education from boarding school Birla Balika Vidyapeeth in Pilani Rajasthan, and then she got enrolled in Miranda House of Delhi University for her graduation in Arts.

Shakti obtained her degree of post-graduation from St’ Xavier College Mumbai. She develops an immense interest in dancing at an early age; she got her dancing training from Terence Lewis Professional Training Institute.

Shakti Mohan Professional Information

DID Star Shakti trained in dance at Terence Lewis dancing Institute, in 2009 she started her professional career in dancing by appearing in the dance reality show Dance India Dance Season 2, luckily she won the dancing show and garnered media attention.

  • Miss Mohan played the lead actress in a series titled Dil Dosti Dance. She was the lead actress of this show.
  • Shakti participated as a contestant in the dance reality show Nachle Ve with Saroj Khan and Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa.
  • She also worked as an assistant choreographer for the famous song Kamli from the movie Dhoom 3, the song was picturized on Katrina Kaif.
  • Shakti established herself as a leading choreographer in Television and film industry. She appeared as a judge, mentor, guest, and host for many dance-related reality shows.
  • Dance India Dance Star is now one of the most talented choreographers of Bollywood. She choreographed songs for celebrities like Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Shahid Kapoor, Katrina Kaif, Jacqueline Fernandez, etc.

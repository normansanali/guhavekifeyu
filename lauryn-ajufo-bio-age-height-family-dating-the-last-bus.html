Meet actress Lauryn Ajufo.

Lauryn is the star of the new Netflix show The Last Bus. So, fans who know her are eager to know her on a personal level. So, here you’ll learn about her height, family, and dating life. Keep scrolling down this article to learn more about her.

Lauryn Ajufo On The Last Bus

Streaming giant Netflix is offering viewers an original TV show titled The Last Bus. The show premiered on the streaming website on 1 April 2022. As a synopsis of the show Netflix shared, “After embarking on a life-changing field trip, a group of whip-smart students fights to save humanity from an army of ruthless drones.”

The 10-part British science-fiction comedy series is about a bunch of witty teenagers on a school trip who band together to survive a robot apocalypse. Drew Casson, Lawrence Gough, Steve Hughes, and Nour Wazzi co-directed the show, which was created and written by Paul Neafcy.

In the show, actress Lauryn Ajufo plays the role of Misha. Other casts joining her are Robert Sheehan as Dalton Monkhouse, Nathanael Saleh as Josh, Moosa Mostafa as Nas, Daniel Frogson as Tom, Carys John as Bethan, Amber-Rose Perry as Borb Puppeteer, Phoebe De Silva as Sophie, Marlie Morrelle as Chelsea, Tom Basden as Mr. Short, Curtis Kantsa as Danny, Laura Crowhurst as Frank, and Lara McDonnell as Lucy

A few days before the show’s premiere, Lauryn posted on her Instagram, “Me because @wildseedstudios The Last Bus comes out on @netflixuk Friday, April 1st”.

Lauryn Ajufo Career

Lauryn started her career as an actress in 2019 with a short film titled Boiling Point. She also appeared on Holby City in 2020 as Steph Shaw in one episode of the show. Furthermore, she appeared in the films Villain (2020) and Boiling Point (2021). Her recent project is The Last Bus, whereas she is also appearing on Accused and ITV2’s upcoming coming-of-age show Tell Me Everything.

Her fellow Boiling Point star Stephen Graham said about her: “I think she is one of the kindest, sweetest young women I have ever met, but as a talent she is phenomenal. She was a joy to have on set and a joy to work with.”

In 2021, Lauryn won the British Independent Film Awards 2021 for her role in the category of Breakthrough Performance in Boiling Point.

The actress is represented by Independent Talent (@ITG_Ltd). She joined the talent agency in March 2022.

Besides acting, Lauryn is also pursuing a career as an R&B musician. She released her R&B single “Brand New” on Spotify in 2017 when she was 16 years old. She is a Brit School student who was also a member of the Oliver Award-winning dance crew Boy Blue Entertainment.

Lauryn was also working on a 5-track EP titled Next Generation. You can also check her out on YouTube.

Fun Fact: Lauryn considers herself a sneakerhead. Her favorite sneakers are Nike Dunks and Nike Zoom Air Fire.

Lauryn Ajufo Family

Lauryn Ajufo was born to her father Jeff Ajufo. Her family hailed from Nigeria.

Jeff is a musician. According to his LinkedIn, he worked as the CEO of Nodough Records from 1998 to 2006. He also worked as a production staff – Britain and Ireland’s Next Top Model in 2013. He also served as the production staff for Betty TV in 2013.

Furthermore, Jeff worked with Capital Dreams Pictures as a Production Manager. He has been working as a music producer at Masterstroke Entertainment since September 2012. He was responsible for the writing and production of Lauryn’s song Brand New.

Likewise, Jiff posted on his Facebook, “Last night my daughter Lauryn Ajufo made her tv debut on BBC’s Holby City. Check it out! #prouddad“.

Not much is known about Lauryn mother, but she has tweeted, “Aunty somewhere is frying meat and blending peppers rn for tomorrow”. The tweet was followed by another tweet that read, “My mum, she is Aunty.”

Lauryn has a sister named Shana Ajufo (@shanaajufo). She is also a singer who released her son Crush On You in April 2020, also produced by her father.

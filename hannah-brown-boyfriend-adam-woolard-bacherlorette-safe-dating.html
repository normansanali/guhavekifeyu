The Real Reason Hannah Brown Is Dating Adam Woolard

September 2024 · 4 minute read

While The Bachelor franchise isn't perfect, we can definitely see why people decide to go on a reality TV show to try to find love. Whether we try to meet people in the real world or we're using several dating apps, it can be really tough to make a real connection. When we're single for a long time, we always hear supportive friends and family telling us to just keep going... but it can feel like we're never going to meet our soulmate.

While fans don't love everyone who stars on The Bachelor or The Bachelorette, and fans don't like Clare Crawley, Hannah Brown has been more popular. She's been really honest about the hard times and now that she has a new boyfriend, it seems like things are looking up in her world. Why was Hannah attracted to her new boyfriend Adam? Keep reading to find out the real reason why Hannah Brown is dating Adam Woolard.

Hannah Brown And Adam Woolard Have A Great Relationship

Fans loved Hannah Brown's YouTube video about her boyfriend, and everything that Hannah has said about the new person in her life has been very sweet.

Hannah Brown shared the reason why she's dating Adam Woolard and it's adorable and swoon-worthy.

According to People, Hannah said that Adam makes her feel totally safe, which is something that she didn't feel in her previous relationships. Hannah noted that Adam is a very chill person and she said, "I've always longed for safety in relationships but I've put myself in unsafe positions. Adam keeps showing up. And he always makes me feel so safe." If any of us have found The One after a serious of bad relationships, we can definitely relate to what Hannah Brown is saying about Adam Woolard.

People reported that Hannah Brown and Adam Woolard swiped on each other on a dating app and are celebrating a year of dating.

Hannah also said that Adam is secure and self-assured and she loves that about him. When talking about Adam, Hannah definitely sounds like a person who is totally in love and who knows that she has found someone special. Hannah said, "He knows himself and I think that's the biggest thing. He's very confident in what he deserves and what he wants to give someone else. He's genuine and he's truly one of the best humans I've ever met. And he is so cute!"

Adam Woolard is a meditation coach as well as a model, so he seems like a very peaceful, wise person, and he often gets philosophical on his Instagram account. Adam shared a photo of him and Hannah Brown and he wrote in the caption, “Life tends to assume a flow state when values and purpose are aligned."

Adam posted a romantic message for Hannah's birthday in September 2021, writing in the caption that he loves her smile and that he loves being part of her life. It seems like a stable, healthy relationship.

Many fans shared on Reddit that they're excited for Adam and Hannah. One wrote, "Wow they look super happy together. Good for them!" Another fan posted, "He’s cute! They look good together, and she looks happy. That’s nice."

There have been a lot of juicy details that have come out about Hannah Brown and her ex-fiance, Jed Wyatt, and while everyone is glad to see Hannah doing so well, it's hard not to want to hear more details about her romantic past. Jed said that the producers wanted him to break up with Hannah on camera and he explained, “The show absolutely insisted that, you know, I hash it out with [Hannah] in person and this and that, and give it to the public so that they can come at me and totally just roast me," according to Us Weekly.

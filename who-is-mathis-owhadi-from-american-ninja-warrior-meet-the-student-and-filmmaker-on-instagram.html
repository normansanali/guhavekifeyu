Who Is Mathis Owhadi From American Ninja Warrior? Wikipedia Bio

Mathis Owhadi is a filmmaker, podcast host, and business student who goes by the nickname “The Kid.” Owhadi is of French origin and speaks French proficiently.

He was initially spotted in the audience cheering on James McGrath during the American Ninja Warrior 8 Atlanta finals and Nate Burkhalter during American Ninja Warrior 9 in San Antonio.

Owhadi originally appeared as a part of the Houston Cougars squad on Team Ninja Warrior: College Madness. When his partner, Zach Tamayo, failed the Salmon Ladder, his team fell to the Georgia Bulldogs in the relay match.

Likewise, he later participated as a member of Team Iron Grip, commanded by Daniel Gil, on American Ninja Warrior: Ninja Vs. Ninja.

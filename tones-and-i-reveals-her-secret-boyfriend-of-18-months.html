Tones and I reveals her secret boyfriend of 18 months

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Tones and I has revealed that she has a secret boyfriend – footballer Jimmy Bedford. 

Appearing on The Kyle and Jackie O Show on Friday, the fiercely private singer, whose real name is Toni Watson, confessed the pair have been dating for the past 18 months.

Jimmy Bedford – nicknamed ‘Bedders’ – is a former brickie and player for the Victorian AFL team, the Frankston Bombers, who now follows the Dance Monkey hitmaker around the world on tour, as her stage technician.

‘He was a brickie [bricklayer], and now he’s my stage tech. In the last year and a half, he’s gone to university, studied, got his Bachelors. 

‘He comes on tour with me as my stage tech. He lives with us. He plays footy for Frankston Bombers. He’s my best friend, he’s hilarious,’ she said.

Laura Bell Bundy Net Worth

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Laura Bell Bundy:

$1.9 million

Laura Bell Bundy is an American actress. She’s also a singer. She started her acting career in the 1990s. During this period, she appeared in the films The Adventures of Huck Finn, Life with Mikey, and Jumanji. She made her first television role in Home Improvement. In the 2000s, she first played Marah Lewis in the soap opera Guiding Light. She later had roles in the movies Surf School and Dreamgirls. In the 2010s, her film credits include Watercolor Postcards and After the Reality. She also played Shelby in the series Hart of Dixie. She became further known for starring as Dr. Jordan Denby in the show Anger Management.

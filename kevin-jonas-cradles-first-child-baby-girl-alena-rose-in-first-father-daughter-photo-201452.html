Kevin Jonas Cradles First Child, Baby Girl Alena Rose in New Photo

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Proud papa! Kevin Jonas may have been excited to live tweet the birth of his first child, but he looked like he was in pure heaven when he got to hold her for the very first time. Dreft laundry detergent, which announced the birth of his and wife Danielle's baby girl on Feb 2, shared the first photo of the father-daughter duo on Wednesday, Feb. 5.

"Alena Rose looks like a little star in her daddy's arms. @KevinJonas #BabyJonas," the company tweeted. In the snapshot, Jonas, 26, is cradling his newborn as she's wrapped in a blanket and wearing a pink cap. "My beautiful girl so proud to be a dad. Alena Rose has stolen my heart," he wrote.

On Feb 2, Jonas and Danielle, 26, welcomed their little bundle of joy into the world. "#babyjonas is on the way!! Follow dreft for all the updates!!!!! It's showtime #thisisnotadrill," the musician tweeted at the start of the 8-hour delivery. "He we go we're pushing!!!! #babyjonas."

That same day, two other family members were cheering about the arrival — Alena's famous uncles Joe and Nick Jonas! "As one life ends, another begins," Kevin's brother Joe, 24, tweeted. "Incredibly happy to be an uncle for the first time. Welcome to this world my beautiful niece."

"My grandfathers memorial was yesterday," Nick, 21, explained further. "Today we welcome a new addition to the jonas family. God is great. Can't wait to meet my new niece." (The Jonas brothers announced via Twitter on Jan. 28 that their grandfather, Jerry Miller, had passed away. Each wrote heartfelt messages, but no other information was provided.)

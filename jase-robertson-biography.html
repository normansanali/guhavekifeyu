Who is Jase Robertson?

Jase Robertson is an American reality TV personality, businessman, and philanthropist.

He is well-known for his work in the reality TV show, Duck Dynasty, and as the previous COO of the business, Duck Commander.

Jase Robertson: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Childhood, Ethnicity

Jase Roberton was born Jason Silas Robertson in Bernice, Louisiana, USA, on August 16, 1969. As of 2022, his age is 53.

He was born to parents, Phil Alexander Robertson (father) and Marsha Kay Robertson (mother). His middle name, Silas is after his uncle, Silas Robertson, his father’s brother.

Jase Robertson has three brothers, Alan, Willie, and Jeptha. During his childhood days, he used to go with his father on hunting expeditions.

Since the age of eight, he wanted to spend a lot of time in the swamps and forests with his father and even missed a lot of school days.

His ethnicity is Scottish and Irish.

Education: School/College, University

Talking about his education, Jase Robertson attended West Monroe High School.

Jase Robertson: Net Worth, Salary

As of 2022, Jase Robertson has an estimated net worth of around $13 million. He has starred in Duck Dynasty since its first show and now the show has already aired 11 seasons.

Jase Robertson has made a good amount of money from the show. Moreover, he also used to be the COO of Duck Commander. He currently works on Wall Street.

An average American TV star makes around $45k a year.

Jase Robertson: Professional Life, Career

Jase started his career by joining his father’s business. He started by fabricating duck calls and managing the quality. Eventually, with the business booming, he went on to become the C. O. O. of the business.

He is famous for taking just two to three minutes to make a duck call. Moreover, he invented the ‘Triple Threat’ call in which he successfully utilizes three reeds instead of two.

He started gaining fame and recognition after appearing on the reality show, ‘Duck Commander. The show featured the Robertson family. However, Jase at first was against the idea of making a show and believed that a reality TV show featuring the duck call business would not succeed.

The show became a hit and its spin-off ‘Buck Commander Protected by Under Armor was released in 2011. It became A&E’s reality show and since then became a huge hit.

The show debuted on March 21, 2012, with an episode titled ‘Family Funny Business. Since then, ten more seasons were released of the show. On March 29, 2017, the final episode, ‘End Of An Era’ was aired.

Currently, Jase Robertson works on Wall Street.

Rumors and Controversy

Although Jase Robertson has not been directly involved in controversies, Duck Dynasty has been somewhat controversial throughout the year.

On December 18, 2013, A&E suspended Jase’s father, Phil from the show for the remarks he made in an interview with GQ Magazine. During the interview for the magazine’s January 2014 issue, ‘What the Duck?’, the interviewer asked him what in his mind was sinful. He replied:

“Start with homosexual behavior and just morph out from there. Bestiality, sleeping around with this woman and that woman and that woman and those men.”

For these comments, he faced a lot of criticism from different individuals as anti-LGBTQ, homophobe, and bigoted.

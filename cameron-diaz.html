Cameron Diaz - Biography

September 2024 · 2 minute read

The Charlie's Angels actress made her film debut with The Mask, and became a household name thanks to roles in My Best Friend's Wedding, There's Something About Mary, and more

    Cameron Diaz' Early Life

    Born August 30, 1972 in San Diego, California, Cameron was raised in Los Angeles, the daughter of a Cuban-American father, Emilio, and Anglo-German mother, Billie.

    Her time in high-school foreshadowed her star-studded future. She was friends with Snoop Dogg, and at 16, signed a modeling contract with Elite Model Management, through which she landed campaigns for Calvin Klein, Levi's, Coca-Cola, and more, plus she was featured on the cover of the July 1990 issue of Seventeen magazine.

    Cameron Diaz' Acting Career

    Cameron made her film debut, and subsequently became a household name, when she was 19 and starred opposite Jim Carrey in one of the top ten highest-grossing films of 1994.

    Cameron Diaz' Personal Life

    Throughout the years, Cameron has been romantically linked to Matt Dillon, Jared Letto, Justin Timberlake, and Alex Rodríguez.

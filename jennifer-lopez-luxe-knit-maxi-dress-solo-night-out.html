Jennifer Lopez Does Summer Luxury in a Heavenly Knit Maxi Dress

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Knits aren’t just a staple material for winter or fall—they’re a great way to transition into the warmer months, too.

Jennifer Lopez proved just how summer-ready her knit pieces can be with a recent solo outing to Giorgio Baldi, the celeb-favored Italian restaurant in Los Angeles. For the occasion, she wore a luxurious maxi dress with a chevron knit pattern from Song of Style. The piece featured a classy boat neckline and flared boho long sleeves.

She styled the look simply, tying it off with some monochromatic pieces like a cream crocodile Hermès Birkin bag and ivory velvet platform heels by Elie Saab. As for her jewelry, she kept things simple with her go-to hoop earrings and her megawatt 8.5-carat green diamond engagement ring.

The Atlas star’s outing comes amid increased speculation over the status of her relationship with husband Ben Affleck, with recent reports claiming the two are experiencing marital issues.

On the same day Lopez headed for dinner at Giorgio Baldi, TMZ reported that the couple had listed the $60.8 million Beverly Hills mansion they purchased together just last year for sale. The news comes after People confirmed in mid-May that Affleck and Lopez are currently living under separate roofs.

“Jen and Ben are continuing to take space from each other,” one source explained to Entertainment Tonight. “They’ve been having issues for a few months and trying to figure things out on their own.”

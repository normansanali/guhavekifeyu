Expressing Gratitude on Her Birthday: 2faces Baby Mama, Pero Adeniyi

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Today marks the birthday of Pero Osaiyemi Adeniyi, the mother of the renowned musician, 2baba Idibia.

On her Instagram profile, the mother of four shared new pictures of herself while expressing her gratitude to the Almighty.

Pero acknowledged that it has always been God, and she will always be thankful to Him.

In her message of thanks, she also wished herself a happy birthday, stating, “It has been you, Lord, and it will forever be you! THANK YOU, LORD! Happy birthday to ME!”

Celebrating Her Daughter’s Special Day: Pero Adeniyi’s Message to Ameerah on Her 22nd Birthday

Elsewhere, Pero Adeniyi, the mother of three of 2baba’s seven children, is celebrating the birthday of her first child from another relationship.

On her Instagram page, Pero shared a video collage of the celebrant and expressed how blessed she feels because of God’s faithfulness.

Reflecting on becoming Ameerah’s mother 22 years ago, Pero emphasized God’s continuous presence in their lives.

She offered prayers for her daughter and conveyed her pride in the woman she is becoming.

In her heartfelt message, she said, “22 years ago, I became your mum, and God has never left us. Ameerah God will continue to lead you, I am super proud of the young lady you have become. God will never depart from you, you will live long and fulfill destiny. Go ahead, it’s your time, my baby!! Love you. Tunro Tunro! My Ace sibe sibe Oluwa dara SI wa! God has been indeed faithful, I am beyond blessed! Happy birthday Ameerah Oluwatumininu!”

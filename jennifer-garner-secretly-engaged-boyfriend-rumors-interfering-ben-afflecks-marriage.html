Jennifer Garner could be walking down the aisle again, as a new report claims the actress is engaged to her long-time boyfriend John Miller.

“John spent a long time making Jen an engagement ring and gave it to her two years ago, around April 2022, which is when she turned 50,” a source told Life & Style, acknowledging that Garner has been spotted with what seemed to be an engagement ring on multiple occasions in recent years, though she’s never confirmed what the ring represents.

Ben And J.Lo Allegedly Won’t Be Invited To Garner’s Wedding

The source said that Garner and Miller want something small and private. “John has land in Malibu,” they continued. “It might happen there.”

However, don’t expect her ex-husband Ben Affleck or his new wife Jennifer Lopez to be on the guest list. “It would be awkward to have Ben at the wedding because Jen would have to invite J.Lo. And Jen and John don’t want to invite any more publicity than necessary, they want no distractions,” they continued.

“This is their special day. They’re much more low-key and want to keep their wedding private,” the source added.

Garner has a net worth of $80 million, and she’s apparently insisting on a prenup if she walks down the aisle for a third time. But the source says John doesn’t have a problem signing one before their nuptials.

Why Jennifer Garner Allegedly Makes J.Lo Uncomfortable

If the reports are true that Garner is engaged, Lopez may be breathing a sigh of relief, as reports have long claimed J.Lo is uncomfortable with how close Affleck has remained to his ex-wife.

One report from last year claimed Lopez insisted on couple’s therapy after she was bothered by paparazzi photos showing Garner and Affleck embracing. “Ben is first to admit the only thing that keeps Jennifer and him together is therapy,” a source told Closer Magazine.

More recently, it was speculated that Lopez may have a problem with how much Garner is relying on Affleck after losing her father earlier this year. One source said that while Affleck is helping his ex-wife grieve, things are going smoothly between everyone involved in co-parenting their blended family.

“They are doing well and are very happy together,” they explained. “Their kids bring them even closer together and keep them humble. Jen and Ben are supportive of one another and just want the best for their relationship and family.”

The source added, “Their kids love being a blended family and mesh well together. They're all kind and get along well."

If Garner is actually engaged, then it’s all the more reason to assume things are going well in their big, blended family.

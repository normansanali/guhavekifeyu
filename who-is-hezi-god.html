Ryan "Hezi God" Carter, the basketball influencer known for his viral presence, a deadly scoring ability, and elite shooting skills, had humble beginnings as a two-sport athlete at an HBCU. Before gaining fame as "Hezi God," Carter was a standout player in both basketball and football at Harding High School in Charlotte, North Carolina. He continued to excel in both sports as he pursued his education at Johnson C. Smith University (JCSU). During his time at JCSU, Carter was a significant contributor to the basketball program, being part of two CIAA championship teams and showcasing his skills on the court.

Dual-Sport Athlete and Success at JCSU 

At Johnson C. Smith University, Carter not only made a mark in basketball but also displayed his talent on the football field. His all-around athletic ability was evident as he continued to pursue both sports at the collegiate level. Carter's success was highlighted by his contributions to the basketball program, where he played under the guidance of Stephen Joyner Sr., and his impressive performances on the football field, where he managed to excel as a quarterback.

Rise to Prominence as "Hezi God" 

After his collegiate career, Carter transitioned to pursue his passion for basketball, ultimately gaining widespread recognition as "Hezi God." His journey to becoming a basketball influencer was marked by his exceptional dribbling skills and scoring prowess. Notably, Carter's viral moments and remarkable performances, including a 103-point game in an American Basketball Association matchup, solidified his status as a prominent figure in the basketball community.

BIG3 Basketball and Professional Success 

Carter's entry into the BIG3 league marked a significant milestone in his basketball career. The league, known for its competitive environment and high-level professional players, provided Carter with a platform to showcase his skills on a larger stage. His impact in the BIG3 was immediate, with his exceptional scoring ability and elite shooting skills earning him recognition as an elite shooter at the professional level. His performances in the BIG3 league, including setting a single-game scoring record and earning consecutive Player of the Week awards, further solidified his position as a formidable talent in the basketball world.

Impact and Recognition in the BIG3 

Carter's emergence as a standout player in the BIG3 league garnered widespread attention and recognition. His ability to combine a stellar handle, muscular frame, fearlessness inside, and deadly shooting prowess made him a force to be reckoned with on the court. His impact was pivotal in leading his team to a playoff run, despite the absence of key players, showcasing his ability to elevate the performance of his team at crucial junctures.

Ryan "Hezi God" Carter's rise to prominence in the BIG3 is proof that he is more than just a celebrity influencer hooper and really does have some serious game. Recently Hezi God was featured on Pro Connect a platform helping athletes get professional jobs overseas and it was noted they said he has some serious potential to play high level overseas basketball.

Return to Hometown Glory 

The culmination of Carter's journey as "Hezi God" was marked by a homecoming to his hometown, where he had the opportunity to display his skills in front of family and friends at the Spectrum Center. His return to the arena where he had previously won CIAA championships served as a testament to his growth and development as a player. Carter's journey from being a hometown hero to achieving recognition on a global scale exemplified his dedication to the sport and his ability to overcome challenges to reach the pinnacle of success.

Growing His Brand

Ryan "Hezi God" Carter's transformation from a two-sport athlete at an HBCU to a basketball influencer in the BIG3 league is a testament to his unwavering dedication, exceptional talent, and relentless pursuit of excellence. His journey showcases the impact of hard work, perseverance, and a relentless passion for the game. As "Hezi God," Carter has left an indelible mark on the basketball community, inspiring aspiring players and earning recognition as an elite shooter and a deadly scorer at the professional level. His story serves as a compelling narrative of triumph, resilience, and the enduring spirit of a true basketball influencer.

Paxton Newell: Death &amp; Obituary Of A Lacrosse Star

The Salt Lake Valley community is mourning the loss of Paxton Newell, a 17-year-old lacrosse player from Corner Canyon High School who passed away earlier this week.

Let’s take a closer look at who Paxton Newell was, what happened to him, the cause of his death, and his obituary.

We will also explore how his community is rallying around him in these difficult times.

Who Was Paxton Newell?

Paxton Newell was a student at Corner Canyon High School in Utah. He was a talented lacrosse player who was beloved by his teammates and coaches. 

He was known for his infectious smile and his ability to make friends with everyone he met. 

Moreover, he was also a dedicated student who cared deeply about his education.

What Happened to Paxton Newell?

Paxton Newell was diagnosed with brain cancer during his sophomore year of high school. 

He underwent treatment, including radiation, to fight the cancer. 

Despite his illness, he remained positive and continued to play lacrosse. He was an inspiration to his classmates, who admired his courage and resilience.

Paxton Newell Cause of Death

Paxton Newell passed away on Tuesday, September 21, 2021, after a prolonged battle with brain cancer. 

His passing has left his fellow classmates and the Salt Lake Valley community heartbroken.

Paxton Newell Obituary

Paxton Newell’s obituary has not been released to the public yet. 

However, his school and community are mourning his passing and have been sharing messages of support and condolences for his family.

Community Honors Paxton Newell

In the wake of Paxton Newell’s passing, his school and the Salt Lake Valley community are coming together to honor his memory. 

Students from Alta and Hillcrest high schools have shown their support, and many have taken to social media to express their condolences.

Dark Souls 3 Bows, Greatbows, and Crossbows Locations

August 2024 · 2 minute read

In Dark Souls3 different bows can be found, also you’ll be able to get them as reward or create from the bosses’ souls. A big number of all players prefer the Crossbows, Bows and Greatbows to use them against their enemies at the distance without exposing themselves to danger.

Where you can find the bows

Bows have been changed. Different kinds of bows in this game have various damage, attack speed and ranges. They need arrows and sufficient dexterity amount.

Black Bow of Pharis

Effect: Pharis Triple Shot
Location: Dropped by Elder Ghru at Farron Keep

Composite Bow

Effect: Rapid Fire
Location: Shrine Handmaid

Darkmoon Long Bow

Effect: Darkmoon Arrow
Location: Dropped by Aldrich’s Soul at Anor Londo

Dragonrider Bow

Effect: Puncture
Location: Smouldering Lake

Longbow

Effect: Puncture
Location: High Wall of Lothric

Short Bow

Effect: Rapid Fire
Location: Purchase from Greirat

Where you can find the Greatbows

The most powerful type of bows in Dark Souls3 is Greatbow. Your enemies will be knocked off their feet with the only shot.

Dragonslayer Greatbow

Effect: Puncturing Arrow
Location: Irithyll of Boreal Valley

Onislayer Greatbow

Effect: Puncturing Arrow
Location: Profaned Capital

Where you can find the Crossbows

The Crossbows require bolts not arrows. That is why they do more damage including the total one. But you will not be able to infuse the Crossbows with poison. They fire rather faster than analogous bows.

Arbalest

Effect: Tackle
Location: Cathedral of the Deep

Avelyn

Effect: Tackle
Location: Grand Archives

Heavy Crossbow

Effect: Tackle
Location: Dropped by Knight of the Deep in Cathedral of the Deep

Knight’s Crossbow

Effect: Tackle
Location: Dropped by Lothric Knight at Lothric Castle

Light Crossbow

Effect: Tackle
Location: Purchase it from Greirat

Sniper Crossbow

Effect: Tackle
Location: Lothric Castle

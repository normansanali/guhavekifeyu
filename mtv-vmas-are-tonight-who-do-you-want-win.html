MTV VMAs Are Tonight! Who Do You Want to Win?

July 2024 · 3 minute read

The music world has descended on Brooklyn for the 2013 Video Music Awards tonight! For the first time EVER the show will be held at the Barclays Center, and that's not only thing that's new. There's a new category: Best Video With a Social Message (Beyonce and Kelly Clarkson are up for that one). Plus, we're expecting performances by Lady Gaga (to start the show), Katy Perry (to close the show), Miley Cyrus, Macklemore and Ryan Lewis and more. And then there's just all those Moonmen to give out. Scroll down to see the full list of nominees -- including Taylor Swift, Lana Del Rey, Selena Gomez and more -- and watch live on MTV tonight at 9/8c.

Who Is Raven Symone's Wife Miranda Maday? Wedding And Their Relationship Details

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Disney star, Raven Symone, is married now. She tied a knot with her girlfriend-cum-wife Miranda Maday and opened about her wedding on 18 June 2020. She shared a wedding picture on Instagram, which showcased her hanging (embracing) her newlywed partner in a wedding dress.

For caption, she wrote:

"I got married to a woman who understands me from trigger to joy, from breakfast to midnight snack, from stage to home. I love you Mrs. Pearman-Maday! Let’s tear this world a new asshole!!! I’m married NOW."

Her wedding revelation quickly followed a series of congratulating comments from several celebs. Actress Yvette Nicole Brown wrote 'PUMPKIN! Congratulations' on the comment. Similarly, her Cheetah Girls co-star Adrienne Bailon, Bria Murphy, and others followed with congratulations.

Raven also shared a picture of her wedding ring. She revealed that they bought the rings at $50 at the thrift store due to coronavirus. The real wedding rings will arrive in 10 weeks from now. 

Miranda Maday, too shared a few pictures of the wedding ceremony with a lovely cute caption of stars. Similarly, she followed up with a video of the function that featured them sharing an intimate kiss while they exchange the vows.

(©: Instagram/Miranda Maday)

Moreover, Miranda placed the kissing picture on her Instagram story in a wedding dress inside a car.

Now, the Disney actress and her newlywed wife will navigate through the beautiful and romantic pavement of their life. Congratulations to the happy couple.

Know more about Raven Symone's wife, Miranda Maday, below.

Who Is Miranda Maday?

Miranda Maday's bio lies behind the bush. She has scarce information regarding her lifestyle and personal affairs.

As per her LinkedIn account, she is a social media manager, who has worked in the entertainment industry as a personal and executive assistant for more than a decade. 

On the other hand, her wife Raven-Symone is an actress known for her series, including 'That's So Raven' and 'Raven's Home.' Besides that, she starred in Black-ish, The View, and The Masked Singer. Initially, she rose to prominence as a child actor in The Cosby Show.

Miranda and Raven-Symone were in a romantic relationship for a few periods before they exchanged their wedding vows.

Bella Hadid Dances on Set in a Skintight White Latex Midi Dress

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Bella Hadid is back to work and looking more stunning than ever.

The supermodel shared backstage footage yesterday on Instagram from a recent photo shoot with beauty brand Charlotte Tilbury. In the clip, Hadid is seen dancing and playfully singing on set while wearing a skintight latex midi dress in an off-white shade. The shiny piece features thick straps and a sharp scoop neckline.

Hadid wears no accessories with the piece, only voluminous bombshell curls and bronzy goddess makeup. “My @charlottetilbury family. Thank you. I cherish you. I appreciate you. I love you,” she captioned her post.

Hadid returned to modeling earlier this month, after a five-month hiatus due to intensive treatment of her Lyme disease. In a video shared on her TikTok on August 10, she lip-synced to “Sprinter” by Central Cee and Dave while sitting behind the scenes on set. She wore a dainty white top with a frilly lace trim and a front-tie detail, plus matching white short-shorts. Meanwhile, her beauty look included shimmery gold eye shadow and long beachy waves. “First day back on set in 5 months,” the Kin Euphorics co-founder captioned the video.

She had previously opened up about the challenges of living with Lyme disease, and her recent struggle to heal, in a series of Instagram posts that documented her recovery period. “Living in this state, worsening with time and work while trying to make myself, my family and the people who support me, proud, had taken a toll on me in ways I can’t really explain. To be that sad and sick with the most blessings/privilege/opportunity/love around me was quite possibly the most confusing thing ever,” she wrote in a candid post.
